What we teach

The Techsmiths programme is designed by industry-leading engineers, to teach all of the skills you need to contribute to a wide range of projects. Software development is the main focus, and we’ll teach you how to work with some of the most popular languages at the moment – but it doesn’t stop there. You’ll learn how to plan and design solutions, manage projects, test things effectively. Techsmiths also get experience deploying, monitoring and upgrading software – a vital part of turning your great ideas into software that improves the world.

How we teach it

Our 12 week bootcamp gives you the opportunity to work on a number of short projects and training exercises, increasing in scale and complexity over the course of the programme. We have very low student:coach ratios, which means you’ll get lots of 1:1 attention from an expert software developer. We like to work in small groups, collaboratively solving difficult problems and having lots of fun on the way!

What's involved

Our 12 week bootcamp is completely free, and will set you up for a career in the country’s fastest growing sector. You’ll graduate from the bootcamp as a well-rounded developer with a strong portfolio, a well-paying job lined up for you, and all the skills you need to excel as a developer. For the first 15 months of your career, we’ll find you jobs with some of the top technical companies in the country, and provide constant mentoring and support.
