What we teach

The TechSwitch programme, designed by industry-leading engineers, will teach you all the skills you need to contribute to a wide range of projects. Software development is the main focus and we’ll cover the most popular languages in use at the moment – but it doesn’t stop there. You’ll learn how to plan and design solutions, manage projects, and test code effectively. TechSwitchers also gain experience deploying, monitoring and upgrading software – a vital part of turning great ideas into software that improves the world.

How we teach

Our 12-week bootcamp gives you the opportunity to work on a number of short projects and training exercises, increasing in scale and complexity over the course of the programme. We have very low student-to-coach ratios, which means you’ll get lots of one-on-one attention from an expert software developer. We like to work in small groups, collaboratively solving difficult problems and having lots of fun on the way!

What's involved

The bootcamp will set you up for a career in the country’s fastest growing sector. You’ll graduate from the bootcamp as a well-rounded developer with a strong portfolio, a well-paid job lined up for you, and all the skills you need to excel as a developer. For the first 18 months of your career, we’ll secure you roles with some of the top technical companies in the country, and provide constant mentoring and support.
